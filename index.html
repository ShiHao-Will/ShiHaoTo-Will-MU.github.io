Hello There👋! I'm Shi Hao (Will) and I am a MSc student by Research for Computer Science at Maynooth University, Ireland. My research focuses on Formal Methods and Specification for Safety Critical Software Systems, aiming to develop and create to improve Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning based systems for a safe yet dependable safety criticial system with software verification techniques.

I am under the supervision of Prof. Rosemary Monahan and Dr. Hao Wu, whose deep expertise in the field of formal methods and software verification.

I always welcome connections and collaboration on relevant research projects and events. Feel free to reach out, and please do not hesitate to contact me by email or other social platforms!
